## § 2. &nbsp; Research Statement

> *"The hardest problems in ML deployment are not modeling or statistical in nature — they are* ***systems problems:*** *feature representation stability under temporal drift, latency constraints from downstream regulatory systems, and provenance & auditability for financial compliance. These concerns are underexplored in the ML research literature but are increasingly central to ML Systems and Responsible AI as subdisciplines."*

```
▸ Systems Perspective  —  Architected AI pipelines processing multi-TB financial transaction
                          streams for fraud detection at Bank of America. The core insight:
                          correctness, auditability, and efficiency must be designed in from
                          first principles — they don't emerge naturally as systems scale.

▸ Bio-inspired Work    —  NOCT-A-VIS applies owl visual cortex principles (weak-signal
                          denoising, temporal integration, spatial stereopsis) to robust
                          sensing — bridging neuroscience, signal processing, & deep learning.

▸ Doctoral Objectives  —  Formalize practitioner insights into rigorous generalizable
                          frameworks. Seeking faculty bridging ML infrastructure & systems:
                          latency-aware feature stores · automated data quality · certified
                          robustness in deployed models.
```
